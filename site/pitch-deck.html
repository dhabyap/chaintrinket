Problem

Trust in second-hand is broken

❓ No Proof

"I bought this at an event. How do I prove it's mine when I walk out?"

📄 Paper Fades

Receipts get lost, ink fades, QR stickers peel off. No permanent ownership record.

🤝 Trust Barrier

Buyers hesitate at booths — "Is this really authentic? Who owned it before?"

🧩 Crypto Gap

Existing NFT solutions require wallets, gas fees, and tech knowledge. Event-goers just want the bracelet.

The result: fewer sales at events, lower perceived value, and no secondary market for physical accessories.

Solution

One scan, one token

🏷️

Physical Tag

Each item has a unique QR tag. Scan with any phone camera — no app install.

🔗

On-Chain Token

Stellar Soroban contract links every bracelet/ring to one NFT. Owner = owner of the physical item.

📱

Shareable Receipt

Proof of ownership with QR code. Share to X, IG, or WhatsApp — instantly verifiable.

✅ No crypto knowledge needed ✅ Works offline ✅ 3-second finality ✅ <$0.001 per tx
